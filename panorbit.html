literacy A big chunk of India’s GDP is generated through unorganized business sector. And most of the stakeholder in this sector still lags behind in digital literacy. Hence designing and building a product for the Tier 2 & Tier 3 cities as well as users from rural India poses a completely different set of challenges. And that’s exactly what we at Panorbit learnt and solved while building our own product FINPoint. A simple to use billing and inventory software for small businesses.
